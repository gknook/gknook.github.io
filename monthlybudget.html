Monthly budget

Have you tried things like ynab before? That's fair. I found it too much and too automatic. So I created my own monthly budget sheet. It's quite simple and functions all in google spreadsheet.

Download the sheet

I've been using this sheet myself for over half a year now. Through the system I have been able to get a solid grip on my finances.

The goal is to set a budget for each category, and then hold yourself to that budget by keeping track of it. Obviously this is manual, but exactly because it's manual I think that you understand much better what is actually happening to your finances.

In a glance

  • Simple: Fill the sheet in on a daily or weekly basis and you'll see whether you are on track for your budgets.
  • Manual: It's just a spreadsheet after all. You fill in what you want to spend every month, then you fill in your daily expense.
